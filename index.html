About Me

The story behind the data

I'm a Data Science student at Indiana University Indianapolis, graduating Fall 2026 — and I came to this field by a path that's anything but straight. Before returning to school, I spent years working in high-paced restaurant and bar environments where quick thinking, pattern recognition, and reading people were essential every shift.

Then I took time away from work to raise my family. Returning to school as a non-traditional student while managing a household pushed me to work smarter, stay disciplined, and find meaning in every assignment. I bring that same intentionality to every dataset I work with.

I'm drawn to questions that have real-world stakes — understanding how major disruptions affect economies, behaviors, and industries. My work this summer is focused on building a hands-on portfolio that demonstrates not just technical skill, but the ability to ask the right questions of data.
